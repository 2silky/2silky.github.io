# About

I don't believe any HVAC technician chose their career, I believe that they were chosen. It takes specially designed people to perform in one of the most physically and mentally challenging fields in the trades. At Service Guru, we remember that it is our purpose to serve our community by applying our knowledge of science and refrigeration principles. 

Refrigeration systems are very complex machines, and as they evolve they become even more challenging to understand. So, as technicians we must never stop learning and always seek to improve our knowledge. Every Service Guru has a complete understanding of HVAC fundamentals, but will always continue to develop as does technology.

By applying science and fundamentals, we can eliminate guesswork. Throughout our careers, we have gotten used to being the last ones called to the job, but now we would like to be the first ones that you call.
